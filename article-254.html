Toshiba T6400DXC LCD Panel Backlight Replacement

This is part 3 of the Toshiba T6400DXC luggable PC repair series. Even after replacing all the leaking capacitors in the LCD panel there were still problems with it sometimes going dark after power on. This is caused by the aging fluorescent lamps that are used for the backlight.

Instead of trying to find replacement fluorescent tubes and dealing with the dangerous high voltage involved I choose to replace them with modern LED strips instead. On a trip to Japan I found some 15cm strips at Akiba LED that could work, which happens to run at 12V DC.

There are two additional circuit boards inside the T6400DXC screen besides the main panel itself. The board on the left is the inverter to generate the AC voltage for the fluorescent tubes, but the other one is a 24V to 12V converter.
