Citizen V1DA-31A Floppy Drive Repair

This is part 4 of the Toshiba T6400DXC luggable PC repair series. The next step was fixing the internal floppy drive, which is a "Citizen" model V1DA-31A. The drive seemed completely dead, and there were two suspects.

The first suspect is the two 22uF 16V SMD capacitors:


These were leaking so needed replacement regardless, where I used MLCC equivalents in size 1210:


The second suspect and obvious fault was the disintegrated belt which had tied itself up around the motor shaft:


I was not sure about the exact dimensions but found a flat "phi 60 x 0.4 x 1.4" replacement:


The floppy drive came back to life after these fixes.
